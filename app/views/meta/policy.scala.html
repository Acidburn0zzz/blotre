What information do allow you us to collect?

Depending on how you use Blot're, you allow us to collect different kinds of information from or about you.

Things you do and information you provide.

We collect content you provide when you use Blot're, including when you create or share colors.

Things others do and information they provide.

We also collect content and information that other people provide when they use Blot're, including information about you. This includes what you follow and who follows you.

How and why you do things.

We collect information about how you integrate Blot're into your life. For example, if you use Blot're to monitor your toast, we may collect information about toast preferences and toaster usage.

We also collect information about what inspires your usage of Blot're and your choice of color. This may include color sources such as photographs, videos, eyesight, thoughts, and dreams.

Things which you are rumored to have done.

We may collect rumors about your relationship with Blot're from sources including, but are not limited to: acquaintances, coworkers, neighbors, your mom, bathroom wall poetry, folk legends, the Nixon Tapes, and all forms of electromagnetic communication.

Things you may do in the future.

We collect information about how you will behave regarding Blot're in the future. This includes all possible futures, plus futures from parallel universes.
